Can you migrate my existing site without downtime?

Yes! We use a staged migration process designed for zero disruption:

  1. Full site clone: We create a complete copy of your site on our infrastructure
  2. Testing & optimization: Thorough QA testing and performance tuning on the staging environment
  3. DNS cutover: Seamless DNS orchestration during a low-traffic window (typically under 5 minutes propagation)
  4. Parallel monitoring: 72-hour observation period with your old host as fallback

We've completed 200+ migrations with zero unplanned downtime. Your visitors won't notice a thing—except faster load times!

Do you support HIPAA, PCI, or other compliance needs?

Yes, we support various compliance requirements:

  • PCI-DSS: Our WooCommerce environments are configured for PCI-compliant checkout flows. We ensure proper SSL, security headers, and payment gateway configurations.
  • HIPAA: Available on Enterprise plans with Business Associate Agreement (BAA) signing. We configure appropriate access controls, encryption, and audit logging.
  • SOC 2: Our infrastructure runs on Google Cloud's SOC 2 Type II certified platform.
  • GDPR: We support GDPR compliance with data processing agreements and EU region hosting options.

We provide compliance documentation and assist with audit preparation. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.
