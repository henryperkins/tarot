## Photography Guide

### Setup Tips
- **Lighting**: Diffused natural light or soft LED (avoid glare/shadows)
- **Background**: Dark matte surface (black or charcoal gray)
- **Layout**: 4-5 cards per photo (horizontal row) works well
- **Distance**: Fill frame while keeping 5-10px margins
- **Focus**: Sharp, clear, no blur
- **Format**: JPG or PNG, any resolution (will be auto-resized)

### Naming Convention
Upload with any name (e.g., `IMG_XXXX.JPG`). The extraction script will handle splitting and renaming.

### Batch Recommendations
To minimize photos needed:

**Batch 1 - Remaining Major Arcana (7 cards)**
